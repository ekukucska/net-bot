Welcome to NetBot!

I can help you diagnose network issues on your Windows machine. Try these commands:
  • ping 192.168.1.1 - Check if a device is online
  • scan network - Find all devices on your LAN
  • what's my IP? - Get your local IP address
  • check ports 192.168.1.10 - Scan common ports
